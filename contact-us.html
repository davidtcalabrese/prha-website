Contact Information

  • In case of an emergency: move all occupants to a safe and secure location – call 911; stay at that location until responders arrive.
  • If not an emergency: call police non-emergency line at 608-255-2345.
  • Report gas leaks or electrical issues: if an emergency call 911. If not an emergency, contact Madison Gas & Electric at 608-252-7111.
  • Water issues: Madison Water Department 608-266-4561.
  • Building or zoning problems: Madison Building Inspector 608-266-4551 or PRHA Board.
  • Trash issues or large items work order: Madison Streets West 608-266-4681.
  • Illegal parking or abandoned vehicles: must be reported to the city by a PRHA Board member.
  • Other issues or questions: contact PRHA Board by phone 608-628-4045. Or by mail to PO Box 44577, Madison WI 53744 – 4577.

    • Contact the board by email: Click here to bring up email template. Be sure to provide contact info such as email address, property address, and your phone number. All emails remain confidential within the PRHA board.
