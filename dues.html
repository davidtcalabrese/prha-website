Annual Assessments (Dues)

  • PRHA charges an annual assessment (dues) against each and all properties in our area. Charges are billed annually for the current fiscal year, which runs from August 1 through July 31 of the following year.
  • Several pages following, including a “Dues FAQ,” thoroughly outline our rules. PRHA was the first HOA in Madison to offer a “time payment plan” for any owner who may be having financial difficulties. Please review these pages to avoid any difficulties.
  • PRHA may legally file a court action or lien against delinquent properties.
  • PRHA dues are tiered depending on the property’s address Our “Court Rate” includes properties on the eight PRHA-owned courts and on properties facing Park Edge/Park Ridge which have a garage facing on a court since PRHA provides court snow plowing services to these properties.
  • Properties on Park Ridge or Park Edge without a garage on a court pay a much lower rate since they don’t pay for snow plowing or court maintenance. Prospective buyers or realtors may contact us for rates on a particular address.
