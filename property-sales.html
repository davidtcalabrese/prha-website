Property Sales

  • All title companies, attorneys, realtors, or others holding a “closing” in our area are highly encouraged to contact us regarding any property sale for the current data on that property. Please allow seven days for us to reply, but we usually respond sooner.
  • All property transactions require payment of a PRHA $50.00 transfer fee, split equally between buyer and seller at time of closing. Closer must mail payment to PRHA within seven days after closing to our address shown herein.
  • PRHA works closely with the City of Madison Assessor’s Office and other agencies to track sales in our area. PRHA’s billing goes to the current registered owner.
  • In the event our transfer fee was not paid at closing, we will bill the buyer for our fee plus a $50.00 penalty fee for evading our rules. Please don’t force us to take that action.
  • Property values in PRHA area have generally remained good even with the current economic and inflation issues.
